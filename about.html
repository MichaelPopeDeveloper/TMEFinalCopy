About Us



The Founders of TME-Systems and a Core Group of Change Advocates have spent more than Two Decades perfecting a Social Enterprise Business Model that Directly Addresses Social and Economic Inequality Specifically in Disenfranchised Communities. Communities.

TME-Systems does NOT conduct Business as Usual! Our Main Focus is developing Social and Economic Eco-Systems that create Immediate Impact in Local Communities. Innovation is our Motto. Our Directive is Crystal Clear.

Disenfranchised Communities Possess the Social and Economic Power to Force Social and Economic Change. There are More Than ENOUGH Resources (money, people, infrastructure, etc.) currently in Disenfranchised Communities Collectively, to Rebuid Themselves!

What is missing is a coordinated National Plan of Action to Increase the Effectiveness of that Power! The National Task Force (NTF) was created in Response! As True Believers in Equality, our Organization Specializes in Identifying Existing Resources within a Community. (without exception, every community has them)

By Design, It Does Not Matter if the Resources are Large or Small. The NTF Consolidates and Redirects those Resources to Create Community Benefits from the Bottom Up! Revenue Development, Infrastructure, Job Creation and More, at the Grassroots Level! We call it Trickle Up Economics!

Revenue Development Opportunities for Individuals, Organizations and Businesses are all part of our Core Common Sense Solutions ACTION PLAN. This strategy works to IMMEDIATELY Increases Revenue Flow into Local Communities and Supports Long Term Sustainability.

Disenfranchised Communities Currently have the Numbers (140 million affected) and the Spending Power (Trillions of Dollars in 2016)! Common Sense Dictates that Communities Take Advantage of these ASSETS! The NTF Economic Blueprint Maximizes those Assets to the Benefit of Individuals/Communities that Participate on Any Level. Together, we become the Power!
