Social Advocates



Social Advocates are Dedicated Servants that want to take their Participatory Value in the Social Arena to the Next Level. They want to be on the Front Lines Working Directly with People in Need. Advocates include Volunteers, Mentors, Life Coaches, Counselors, Social Workers and Literally Anyone who has a Real Desire to Help People.

If Your Passion is related to Social or Economic Equality, then Your Participation in the NTF can Generate the Economic Stimulus for Your Vision/Mission.

The Need for Social Advocates is so Massive and Growing at such a Rapid Pace that Tens of Thousands of Social Advocates can be Developed Across the Country. NTF Members are Equipped with the Innovation and Resources to Serve this Tremendous “Target Market” at the Grassroots Level.

In The Trenches



While “In the Trenches” Social Advocates can Develop Revenue Sources for Themselves/Organization while Helping to Create Numerous Social Services Vital to a Successful Recovery of this population.

While Social Advocates Are Economic Advocates as well, their Main Focus is Fighting “In the Trenches” with the Ones They Serve. Assisting those at the Grassroots Level with services that Improve their Situation.
